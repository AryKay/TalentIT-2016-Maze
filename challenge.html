TalentIT 2016 Challenge


Welcome to Smarp's mini challenge! Try solving it and receive a reward from us at the TalentIT 2016 fair.

Below is an image of a maze. You need to get from the START point to the END point in only 11 moves. You have two sets of tools to solve the challenge:

  • Single-move: You can move one step at a time using this tool. Keep track of your moves though, as each step counts as one move!
  • While-loop Constructor: You can create while loops depending on a single condition and travel longer distances while saving moves!

Moves left: 11



Single-move
Click on a button to go one step to that direction. Each step counts as ONE move.




While-loop Constructor
First click on 'While', then pick a condition from this box and afterwards pick your desired moves in order from the 'Single-move' commands. Finish the loop by clicking on 'While' again. The loop itself counts as ONE move as well.
